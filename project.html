Zero-Gravity Drone
A way to stabilize a drone in an area where there is a semi-gravity, so where the weight almost doesn't matter. This is our project to take part of the Zero-G flight in France and I was responsible for all the software and the communication of the drone and the computer, even if I was also working on the hardware to build and think the architecture of the drone.
We have been choosen among a huge amount of teams to make and try our project in September of 2020 by the CNES's engineers(National Center of Scientific Research) and I will share to you every steps and advances we make. I will also build really soon a Web Page for our group to show you every details/news about our adventure.
This is a short video of the prototype we built, we started to build it for real and the structure should be finish for the end of June.
